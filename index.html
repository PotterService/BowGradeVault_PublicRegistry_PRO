About this registry

What Bow grading means

Bow Grade Vault is a personal grading and tracking system. Grades, authenticity status, ownership history, and slab details are recorded by the collector/admin and are not the same as third-party grading from PSA, Beckett, CGC, or other companies.

GradeOverall condition score based on centering, corners, edges, and surface.
AuthenticityShows the admin’s authenticity status and verification notes, if provided.
Bow SlabIf slabbed, the card was slabbed by Bow Grade Vault with a Bow cert number.
OwnershipCurrent owner and transfer history may appear when the admin chooses to make it public.
